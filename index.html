Participants must submit a PowerPoint presentation (.ppt, .pptx) or PDF. Maximum of 10 slides (excluding title and thank you slides).

Each team will be given 7 minutes for presentation and 3 minutes for Q&A.

Teams can consist of 1 to 3 members. Inter-college teams are allowed.
