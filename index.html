About the Event

NextGen Computing is a premier technical symposium designed to bring together the brightest minds in technology. Our mission is to provide a platform for students to showcase their research, innovative ideas, and technical prowess in the field of modern computing.

This year, we focus on the convergence of Artificial Intelligence, Quantum Computing, and Blockchain. We invite students from all departments to step forward, present their papers, and contribute to the dialogue that shapes our future.
